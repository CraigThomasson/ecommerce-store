Get to know us.

A clear conscience.

Here at CandleKeep we put the environment and animal welfare fist. This means you can shop with us with a clear conscience. All our products are vegan and produced with a net neutral carbon footprint as a minimum standard.

Top quality.

Our products are not just guild free there also top quality. The candle we produce have a long smooth burn and a steady glow. The scented candles in our range have a subtle scent that is not over baring and will complement any environment you're in.

All natural

Because our products are made of natural plant-based ingredients you can dispose of them with ease. All our candles our biodegradable and our glass, Woodend and bamboo candle holder can all be re-cycled or even up-cycled!
